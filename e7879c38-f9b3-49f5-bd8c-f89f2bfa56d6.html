Description

WEBFISHING is a multiplayer online casual fishing game where you relax, hang out, make friends, and catch fish!

CATCH THEM ALL!

Nearly 100 different creatures to catch- from the smallest snail to the biggest shark- you can catch it! Complete your journal, find rare fish, get loads of money, and get tons of special rewards!

UPGRADE AND CUSTOMIZE!

Become the ultimate fisherman! Upgrade your gear with new baits, lures, and rods- and collect tons of different cosmetics and props to show off your personality!

PLAY WITH FRIENDS!

WEBFISHING is designed around being social and hanging out with friends and strangers in the camp! Meet new people, find hidden spots, make awful fish puns, and catch fish together!

PLAY WITH TOYS!

Play REAL Guitar! Punch your friends! Draw with chalk! There's many tools and toys to play with in the sandbox of WEBFISHING!
