Description

Play as your favourite club or country in Online Seasons or create your own unique squad to play your style of football in Ultimate Team. Challenge your friends or challenge the world – it’s the authentic FIFA experience, now FREE to play on PC.

EA SPORTS™ FIFA World features award-winning EA SPORTS FIFA HD console gameplay and over 30 officially licensed leagues, 600 clubs and 16,000 players.

Key Features

FIFA World Ultimate Team
Build your own unique club that plays your style of football and hit the pitch to compete in online and offline modes. Play the 24/7 transfer market and strategically outwit other gamers in buying, selling and trading the world’s greatest superstars to create your Ultimate Team.

Accessible Controls
Play your way! Casual or core? Try the intuitive mouse based controls that are simple to use, but a challenge to master; or pick up and play with traditional gamepad or classic keyboard controls. The choice is yours!

League Mode
Lead your favorite real-world club to victory in Online Seasons and experience the drama of promotions and relegation as you battle against other community players to climb the league tables and reach the top flight.

EAS FC Social Hub
Capture and share spectacular goals and beautiful moments you create on the pitch and instantly post them to the in-game social feed. Keep up with your friends’ in-game activity and debate and discuss the latest results and highlights with the community.

Match Invites
Challenge your friends to an Ultimate Team showdown and see who has what it takes to rule the pitch.

Tournaments
Enter your Ultimate Team into single player tournaments and challenge football clubs from around the world! Win, and you'll unlock new tournaments and rewards to boost your Ultimate Team.

Complete Authenticity
31 officially licensed leagues, more than 600 clubs and 16,000 players.

Accomplishments
Earn rewards to help your squad on their football journey by completing challenges revolving around gameplay, the transfer market, squad management, prestige and more. Dynamic accomplishments also surface on a regular basis to bring forth new challenges revolving around the latest and greatest moments from the real world of football.
