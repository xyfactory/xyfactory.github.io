Description

 

 

WILD COUNTRY is a fantastical strategy game where players use beautifully illustrated cards to construct buildings and perform abilities to build the best city. Buildings score points, and the first to reach the objective wins!

 

Wild Country combines CCG gameplay with innovative mechanics inspired by classic city-building games to create something unique and approachable. This game is for people looking for something wholesome, comedic, strategic, and full of things to do.

 

Do you have what it takes to win?

Feature Overview

 

  • Build your deck of 30 cards. Each card represents a Building, Ability, or Event with a unique action. The goal of the game is to outsmart and outearn your rival.
  • Play cards to place buildings and earn enough profit, but remember that placing certain building types next to one another can have positive (or negative) effects.
  • There is always something new to collect, learn, and master. There’s a deck for every personality and playstyle, and it’s up to you to discover your winning strategy.
  • Grow your collection and stand out from the crowd! There’s a ton to customize, from mayors, skins, and outfits to flags, emotes, and cards.
  • Embark on a journey like no other! Advance through a campaign bursting with mysteries to discover, quests to uncover, and things to do.
  • Grab your friends to join the chaos with competitive and casual game modes!
  • Don’t be afraid if you’re new to the genre! Anyone can join the fun with intuitive mechanics and customizable gameplay options to suit your needs.
